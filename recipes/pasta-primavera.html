Pasta Primavera

Pasta primavera is an American dish packed with so many vegetables! It is so versatile, that anything you have on stock can be added to make this dish even tastier. Try it out!

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh veggies, such as red onion, carrots, broccoli, bell pepper, yellow squash, zucchini, tomatoes, and garlic
  • 2 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmigiano, divided

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta according to the package directions
    • Reserve 1/2 cup of water before draining pasta.
  2. While the pasta is boiling, cook the vegetables
    • Heat olive oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add red onions and carrots and saute for 2 minutes.
    • Add broccoli and bell pepper and saute for 2 minutes.
    • Add squash and zucchini and saute for 2-3 minutes or until the vegetables have nearly softened.
    • Add garlic and tomatoes, and Italian seasoning and saute for 2 more minutes.
  3. Toss pasta and veggies together with seasonings
    • Mix vegetables and drained pasta togeter in a serving bowl.
    • Drizzle in lemon juie, season with a little more salt, and toss while adding in pasta water to loosen as desired.
    • Toss in 1/4 cup parmigiano and parsley, then serve with remaining parmesan on top.
