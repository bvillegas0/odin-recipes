Green Bean Casserole

Green bean casserole is an American baked dish that was created by Campbell Soup Company, and is popularly served during Thanksgiving dinner. Iconic and simple to make, this dish will leave you feeling full and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 cup of whole-fat miilk
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 4 cups cooked cut green beans
  • 1-1/3 cups French's French Fried Onions

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 350-degrees F.
  2. Fold in the soup, ilk, soy sauce, beans, and 2/3 cup onions in a 1-1/2 quart oven-safe casserole until all ingredients are combined. Season the mixture with salt and pepper.
  3. Bake for 25 minutes or until hot and bubbling. Stir the bean mixture. Sprinkle the remaining 2/3 cup onions.
  4. Bake for another 5 minutes or until the onions are golden brown. Let green beans rest a few minutes before serving.
