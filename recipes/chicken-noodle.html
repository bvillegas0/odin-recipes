One Pot Chicken Noodle Soup

This classic recipe is so simple, yet so delicious. This dish is perfect when you're feeling under the weather or for a simple, hearty meal! You will feel much better in no time, whether you're sick or not.

Ingredients

  • Chicken broth
  • Cream of chicken soup
  • Cooked chicken
  • Vegetables, such as onions, carrots and celery, but you can add any vegetables you like!
  • Dried parsley, black pepper, chicken base
  • Uncooked egg noodles

Steps

  1. Prep: Thinly slice carrots, celery, and finely chop the onions.
  2. One pot: Combine all of the soup ingredients, except for the egg noodles, into a large pot and bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer: Once boiling, cover pot with a lid, and reduce the heat to medium. Let it simmer for 15 minute or until the veggies are softened to your preference.
  4. Egg noodles: Add dry, uncooked egg noodles and let it cook for an additional 10 minutes.
